Tech News

Apple introduced something out of the blue yesterday.
After the pressure from the news about right to repair, Apple has introduced new ways for customers to fix their products
themselves with new unlocks in software and a new self-repair program offered.

This feature was initially released in the developer beta versions of iOS where users have found out that after changing non-genuine screens, the previously locked features of face-id were removed. Meaning Apple is finally giving into right to repair.

Appl's right to repair movement has also been backed up by another program Apple is now offering named Self-Service Repair. This program allows customers to buy genuine apple components and repair their devices themselves. This program is initially being tested in America with other countries joining in 2022.

“Creating greater access to Apple genuine parts gives our customers even more choice if a repair is needed,”
- Jeff Williams, Apple chief operating officer.
